The Magic of Software Engineering

"Magic" is a term used to describe a process whose details are so well hidden that it seems impossible. To me, that is what software engineering is.

As a software engineer, my concern is with managing complexity while keeping it out of view from the audience. Most users need not concern themselves with the data structures, algorithms, and fancy techniques that keep our digital world afloat.

Personally, I am endlessly fascinated by the breadth and depth of the field - there is always more to learn in the production of good software.
